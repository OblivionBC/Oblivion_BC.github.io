About Me

Here you will learn about my athletics, hobbies, and background.

Athletics

I am currently a Nationally ranked track and field athlete, with my main events being shotput and discus. I compete with the Nanaimo Track and Field Club which is very handy since it is directly below VIU residences where I live. When I enjoy something, I find even more joy in mastering it as I do with programming, track is no exception. I powerlift 2-5 days a week and practice 2-3 days a week to improve myself. I also feel that all programmers should find some sport that they like. It is a good way to blow off steam, and keep your body active after sitting at a chair most of the day!

Hobbies

To wind down after a long day of writing proofs and learning, I have a couple things that I like to do. I play video-games. Like I play them a lot. I mentioned earlier that I touched a computer when I was 11, that was to play Minecraft and Overwatch, and I still play both of those games. I love games because I can get exactly what I want out of them. Long day? I can play a relaxing game. Need some adrenaline? Competition or horror game. On top of gaming, I also like to cook a lot. My favourite thing to make currently is any type of sushi with spicy kewpie mayo, so yummy!

Background

I was born on Saltspring Island in 2005. Although it has its charm, there is not a whole lot to do other than exploring the wilderness; and I was not much of a wilderness explorer. I lived there and commuted over a ferry to school until 2016 where my family moved to Duncan where we went to school. I attended Duncan Christian School from grade 7 to grade 12 and graduated in 2022 at 17 years old. I then was accepted into VIU's Computer Science program and moved onto the Student Residences to start my first year of university. Going forward I have been hired as a Community Leader on Student Residence for next year and am hoping to enjoy University as much as I did this year!
