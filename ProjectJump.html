Project Jump, now named Tower of Dave -- is an Unreal Engine project publisihed on itch.io from my software engineering class. As the team member with the most game development experience, I took the role of team leader, and created schedules following a spiral SDLC at first then agile once we they gained experience. I also designed the git workflow, and phases with the deliverables we would create. Tower of Dave is a 2D infinite vertical platformer with procedurally generated levels with obstacles, and some AI renditions of songs sung by our professor.
